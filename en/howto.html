How to Play / 遊びかた

How to Play

A geography quiz where you drag country (or state) names onto a blank map to learn them. It's simple — you can start in three steps.

1

Pick a region

From the region page, choose what you want to play — Europe, Africa, US states, and more.

2

Drag a name

A list of names sits below the map (on mobile) or beside it (on desktop). Grab a name with your finger or mouse and move it onto the map.

3

Drop it in the right place

Release over the correct spot. A correct answer turns the country green and shows its name.

While dragging, a crosshair pointer (🎯) appears just above your finger. Its center is where the drop is checked — so even tiny countries are easy to target.

4

Place them all to win!

Place every name correctly to finish, with a confetti celebration.

Finish with zero mistakes for a special "Region Master" perfect screen. Your time is recorded too, so try again to beat it.

Zoom & pan

If a small country is hard to hit, zoom in on the map.

  • Desktop: the ＋ / − buttons at the bottom-left, or your mouse wheel. Drag the map to pan.
  • Mobile / tablet: pinch with two fingers to zoom, drag with one finger to pan.
  • The ↺ button resets the view.

More tips

  • Releasing a name over the list (outside the map) does not count as a mistake — you can safely try again.
  • Use "Hide labels" to remove the placed names and test yourself.
  • Use "Restart" to start over any time.
